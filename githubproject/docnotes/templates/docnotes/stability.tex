Introduction to Thermodynamic Cycle
\begin{equation}
    \Delta G_1+\Delta G_3=\Delta G_2 + \Delta G_4
\end{equation}    
\begin{equation}
\Delta\Delta G = \Delta G_2 - \Delta G_1 = \Delta G_3 - \Delta G_4
\end{equation}
