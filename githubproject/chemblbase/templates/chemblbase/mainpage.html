CMDB

Welcome to CMDB (Compound Management DataBase), a public access service for computational ligand discovery. CMDB contains tools to help you manage the in-house compounds: you can upload and download the structure, record the realated assays and predict useful properties

CMDB is provided by Xiangyu Jia, senior expert in CADD and AIDD. Xiangyu Jia has done business with Silicon Therapeutics, Simcere and OTSUKA.

The current version of CMDB is not open sourced. For now, to use CMDB, please contact Xiangyu Jia by xychina.jia@gmail.com

To use CMDB, you must create an account and log in.

Caveat Emptor: We do not guarantee the quality of any calculation for any purpose and take no responsibility for errors arising from the use of this system. CMDB is provided in the hope that it will be useful, but you must use it at your own risk.

CMDB is a work in progress. (July 31, 2022). Any suggestion will be appreciated.
